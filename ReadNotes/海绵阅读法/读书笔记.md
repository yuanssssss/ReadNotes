## 💡 阅读动机 | Reading Motivation

我为什么要读这本书？市面上有那么多书，我为什么翻开了这本？是什么让我对这本书感兴趣？我对这本书有什么期待？我想从书里得到什么？这本书能给我什么？

**Why do I want to read this book? There are so many books on the market, so why did I pick up this one? What makes me interested in this book? What are my expectations? What do I want to gain from it? What can this book give me?**

一开始大概是被这本书的名字所吸引吧，有听说过元编程的概念，应该在Missing Semester和Cpp的文档里面看到过，大概就是操作代码的代码，比如说宏，看到这个"为了阅读的阅读"，然后就有一点好奇吧。

**Initially, I was attracted by the title of the book. I had heard about the concept of meta-programming before, probably seen in Missing Semester and C++ documentation. It's basically code that operates on code, like macros. This sparked my curiosity about "reading for the sake of reading" itself.**

其次就是想获得一种阅读的新的方法吧，看到书名就知道是讲阅读方法的，是在我阅读《亲密关系》过程中发现自己读了容易忘记，然后微信读书偶然间推荐了这本书。

**Secondly, I wanted to learn a new reading method. From the title, I could see it's about reading strategies. I discovered during reading "Intimate Relationships" that I tend to forget what I've read, and then WeChat Reading happened to recommend this book.**

期待的话，就是能够改善我的阅读情况，或者能够提供一个新的认识，就是那种"哇，原来是这样的啊"的感觉。

**My expectation is to improve my reading habits or gain new insights — that kind of "wow, so that's how it works" realization.**
